GPU Miner Weekly Reward Model

1,600,000 RAI distributed weekly using a fairness-first contribution scoring system.

Legend of Score Variables

Abbreviation Meaning
Sub Jobs Submitted
CrC Creation Completed
EfC Effort Completed (processed jobs)
SR Success Rate
JC Job Creation Score
Eff Effort Score
Pres Presence Score
Steady Steadiness Score
Help Helpfulness Score
Build Validator Builder Bonus

The Six Contribution Scores

Job Creation Score

JC = 1 + min(0.08 , completion_rate × 0.08)

Effort Score

Eff = √EfC × SR

Presence Score

Pres = active_hours / 168 × SR

Steadiness Score

Steady = (a_d × l_s_d) / 49

Helpfulness Score

Help = 1 + hours / 24 × 0.25 × SR

Builder Bonus

Build = 1 + uptime × 0.05 + share × 0.05

Final Contribution Formula

Final = Eff × JC × Pres × Steady × Help × Build

Determines each wallet’s share of the weekly 1,600,000 RAI pool.
